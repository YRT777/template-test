Higher Diploma in Cybersecurity (Testing and Compliance)

Introduction

Promotion of innovation and technology is everywhere nowadays as internet services are becoming more popular and facilities are getting more advanced. Individuals, companies and all aspects of our society are enjoying the benefits brought by this new global norm. Yet, people have to face the challenges such as risks and threats that come with the advantages, as the number and categories of cyberattacks and cybercrimes keeps on surging. To combat against these issues, the HKSAR government is considering to enact a new law in this respect, as our Chief Executive has announced earlier in her Policy Address. The aforementioned legislation, once executed, can raise the standard of cybersecurity in Hong Kong and offer better holistic protection to Hong Kong and its citizens in this respect. Cybersecurity has come of age! As a forward-looking vocational and professional education and training (VPET) provider, HKCT launches a new Higher Diploma in Cybersecurity to meet the demand for talents in this profession.

Programme Features

  • Funding support: HKCT successfully sought around HK$20 million subsidy from ESGS (Enhancement and Start-up Grant Scheme for Self-financing Post-secondary Education) to develop this Programme and equip it with professional hardware and system platforms in order to provide students with the latest and vocational teaching & learning facilities.
  • State-of-the-art Cyber Labs: The newly established ¡§Security Operations Centre¡¨ and ¡§Cyber Range & Practice Centre¡¨ allow a wide range of teaching and learning activities such as scene simulation, competition, research, testing, drill and exercise. Students in these Centres can interact with virtual and cyber world status in real-time for experiments and training, such as network attack and defense, network invasion and maintenance, and even the embedment and detection of Trojan worm.
  • Cyber Range Interconnection: The cyber range has also achieved cyber range interconnection with the Yellow Crane Cyber Range of Huazhong University of Science and Technology, the Industrial Control Cyber Security Range of Guilin University of Electronic Technology, and the Jiaxing Cyber Range of Zhejiang University, which are located in the national network security base will help cooperate online simulation tests and competitions.
  • Strong Industry Ties: The programme is supported by renowned local and mainland technology companies including but not limited to China Unicom Global, Topsec Technologies Group, Alibaba Cloud, Sangfor Technologies which will provide exchange and training opportunities, internship and career development to our students in Hong Kong and the Greater Bay Area.
  • Renowned Academic Partners: Co-constructed the "Zhejiang-Hong Kong Cross-Border Data Security Research Center" with "The School of Cyber Science and Technology (SCST) at Zhejiang University" to work together on the talent exchange and development, and join hands to develop professional skills, product manufacturing, research, and technology exchange, etc.
  • Support by HKSAR Bureau: Commendation on facilities received from Innovation, Technology and Industry Bureau (ITIB), Cyber Security and Technology Crime Bureau (CSTCB) and Office of the Government Chief Information Officer (OGCIO) etc.
  • Route to Professionalism: This Programme prepares students who wish to pursue related certificates such as CISSP, CISA and CISP in this profession
